unknown

2020, CAC Lab, Shanghai/Chengdu

UNKNOWN by YIN Xiuzhen is an exhibition developed by CAC Lab team, collaborated with artist YIN Xiuzhen as an Art&Technology program in the Chronus Art Center. It created an awe-striking work that transforms the exigencies of world affairs and flux of emotions into constellations of heavenly bodies, in which fleeting human presences catalyze and transpire a universe of ephemerality and uncertainty.

As a part of the curation team, I contributed to the space design, physical structure design and exhibition production assistance.
