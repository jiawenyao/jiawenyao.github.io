browsing-browser-browsed

a cascading structure on a structure of cascade

2021

A webpage is a hypertext document delivered by a web sever to the user and displayed in a web browser[the software]. Webpage as a metaphor of drawable page sounds and looks flat, but behind this flatness, it is a structure of high hierarchy.

In the webpage of the browsing-browser-browsed, this hierarchy is used as its structure. All the containers, or layers, or <div>s are cascaded rather than laid out, but when it unfolds, the layout follows a prevalence fashion of news websites that an experienced browser [the person] can easily locate the desired type of content based on prior experience.
