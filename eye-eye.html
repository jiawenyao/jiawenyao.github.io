eye-eye

2020, MorrowAgent, Shanghai

This is a commission work for VISTOPIA「看理想」 at Therion Bookstore「神兽之间」, Shanghai.

The webcam captures the eyes in front of it, then enlarge and present them respectively on the left and right screens contained in two black boxes, which echoes the VISTOPIA's logo.

With this work, the viewers can feel a familiar but strange intimacy by looking into their own eyes, and eyes only.
